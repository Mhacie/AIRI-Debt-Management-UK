**AIRI - AI Readiness Index for UK Debt Management**

A quantitative diagnostic tool to assess institutional preparedness for ethical
AI agent deployment in UK debt management institutions, aligned with FCA
Consumer Duty requirements.


**About This Project**

This application serves as the interactive artifact for the master's thesis:

"Modelling Global Readiness For AI Agents In Financial Services:
A UK Debt Management Perspective"

**Features**

* Self-Assessment Survey: 31 indicators across 4 readiness dimensions
* Interactive Dashboard: Real-time visualization of AI readiness scores
* Benchmarking: Compare against industry cohort data
* Actionable Insights: Personalized recommendations by readiness band


**How to Use**

* Visit the live app
* Complete the 31-question survey (0-8 scale)
* View your personalized readiness dashboard
* Explore aggregate analytics

**Framework Dimensions**
* Strategy and Governance - AI strategy, FCA alignment, oversight
* Data and Technology - Infrastructure, MLOps, security
* People and Skills - Leadership, training, culture
* Risk and Ethics - Bias mitigation, explainability, Consumer Duty

**Technical Stack**

* Python 3.11
* Streamlit
* Plotly
* Scikit-learn
* Pandas / NumPy

**Research Methodology**

Design Science Research (DSR) methodology following Peffers et al. (2007)
